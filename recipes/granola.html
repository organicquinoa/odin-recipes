Peanut Butter Granola

About

This is my favorite thing to eat every morning! With some yogurt it is ~chef's kiss~

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • a dash of salt :)

Steps

  1. melt the peanut butter and maple syrup together, then add vanilla
  2. mix into the oats, cinnamon, and salt
  3. put mixture on a sheet pan and bake for 20-25 minutes at 325 degrees
