Lentil Soup!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups red lentils
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 5 cups veggie broth
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • whatever spices ur heart desires

Steps

  1. cut up onion and garlic and sautee in some olive oil in a bigass pot for a few minutes.
  2. add lentils, broth, tomato paste, and spices
  3. bring to a boil then simmer and cover for 15 minutes
  4. open pot and adjust spices and add broth if needed. Cover and allow to simmer for another hour or two.
  5. turn off the heat and mix in lemon juice
