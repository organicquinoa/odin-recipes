Vallatini Beans

Made with love by the baddest bitches of Holliston, Massachusetts. Love u ray

Ingredients

  • 3 cans of black beans
  • 1 yellow onion
  • tons of garlic
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 8 oz veggie broth
  • 2 tsp each of coriander, cumin, chili powder, paprika, and oregano

Steps

  1. Sautee onion and garlic in olive oil in a bigass pot for a few minutes
  2. Add tomato paste, beans, veggie broth, and seasonings. Bring to a boil then cover and simmer for 15 minutes
  3. Uncover and stir, then put cover back on. Stir every few minutes until veggie broth is mostly evaporated and the beans taste good
